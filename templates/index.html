What is Smart Agriculture?

Smart Agriculture is a modern, AI-supported platform built for Tanzanian farmers and businesses. It helps users make informed decisions by analyzing market trends, crop prices, and recommending the best planting strategies.

Key Features

AI Market Trends

View real-time price trends of key Tanzanian crops like cashew, coffee, and sesame.

Smart Crop Suggestions

Get personalized planting recommendations based on region, season, and market data.

Predictive Analytics

Use AI to forecast the best times to plant, harvest, and sell for maximum profit.

Location-Based Insights

Access insights tailored to your exact location to improve productivity.
